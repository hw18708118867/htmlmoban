Award Worthy W3C Standards

World Wide Web Consortium (W3C) have developed industry standard specification and guidelines for web development, to enhance the way web code interacts on the internet. At Vartro, we take this seriously, not only will our code validate for CSS (2.0/3.0), XHTML (1.0) and Accessibility (AA) – we have also based our validation on the Australian Web Awards standards.

We accept all major file formats

As designers, we all specialise in our software of choice, from Adobe Photoshop to Adobe InDesign – we cater for various formats to make your life easier allowing you to produce the best design results without the worry of conversion. Formats include: PSD, IND, JPEG, PNG, PDF, AI, TIFF, EPS and much more.

Image/Server Optimisation

We convert all images to their highest possible quality, giving consideration to file size. We choose the best format and compression that fits the image profile – JPEG, GIF or PNG. Our use of CSS Repetition and CSS Sprites will lower the number of HTTP requests, which will save you money on hosting bandwidth, as well as make your website load faster.
